# Section VI — Firm Qualifications & Experience

## Team Meridient: A Purpose-Built Partnership

Team Meridient is not a general-purpose consulting firm assigned to a leadership project. It is a deliberate partnership formed because this engagement — assessing and developing leadership capacity at a military institute — requires three distinct capabilities that rarely coexist in a single organization: institutional credibility within the military academy system, evidence-based coaching and adult development expertise, and the measurement architecture to make both of those capabilities produce data-driven, compounding results.

Each partner brings a primary capability aligned to a phase of the OODA methodology that structures this engagement.

## Meridient Consulting, LLC — Kim Sullins, Principal

*OODA Role: DECIDE — Institutional navigation, stakeholder alignment, strategic communication*

Meridient Consulting is a certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) and 100% veteran-owned firm specializing in strategic communication, executive messaging, and organizational alignment. Kim Sullins, the firm's founder and principal consultant, is a graduate of the United States Military Academy at West Point.

Kim understands the operating environment at VMI — not as an outside observer, but as someone who was formed by a system with parallel traditions, values, and leadership expectations. She brings the institutional fluency to navigate VMI's culture, build trust with senior leadership, and translate analytical findings into recommendations that land with credibility. Her role in this engagement is to ensure that every assessment insight, every coaching intervention, and every strategic recommendation is presented in language and context that VMI's leadership can act on with confidence.

*OODA Role: ACT + ORIENT — Coaching delivery, vertical development, sensemaking*

Shana Hall Graves brings over 25 years of leadership coaching, management consulting, and organizational change across federal agencies, Fortune 500 companies, and mission-driven organizations. She is an ICF Professional Certified Coach (PCC), Certified Leadership Circle Profile 360 Assessor, and a practitioner of Vertical and Adult Development frameworks (Kegan, Torbert, Cook-Greuter).

Shana currently serves as Adjunct Faculty, Coach Mentor, and Assessor at Georgetown University's Institute for Transformational Leadership. Her prior consulting career includes senior roles at Ernst & Young and PricewaterhouseCoopers, and her client portfolio spans The White House, General Services Administration, Department of Veterans Affairs, Department of Homeland Security, General Electric, Cigna Healthcare, and Teach for America.

Her expertise in vertical development — the study of how leaders grow in their capacity to handle complexity, not just in what they know — is directly aligned with the Neuroflexible dimension of the BANI+ framework. She is the practitioner who will translate assessment data into developmental action: coaching cohorts, facilitating leadership development sessions, and conducting Leadership Circle Profile 360 assessments that provide individual leaders with a deep, evidence-based mirror of their effectiveness.

## Why This Team — Together

The value of Team Meridient is not additive — it is multiplicative. Each partner's strength creates the conditions for the others to be maximally effective:

- Curtis builds the measurement engine that makes invisible patterns visible.
- Shana translates those patterns into developmental action through coaching, assessment, and facilitation.
- Kim ensures that every insight and intervention lands within VMI's culture with the credibility and clarity required for institutional adoption.

No single firm in this partnership could deliver this engagement alone. Together, they form a team purpose-built for exactly what VMI is asking: a rigorous assessment followed by sustained, evidence-based leadership development that is sensitive to the military academy context, grounded in validated methodology, and designed to compound in value across four years.

# Compliance & Legal Notes

## Key Compliance Items
- Anti-discrimination provisions (Federal Civil Rights Act, VA Fair Employment, ADA, VPPA)
- Executive Order 61 (2017) — discrimination prohibition covering sexual orientation, gender identity, political affiliation, veteran status
- Civility in State Workplaces — contractor workers must not engage in harassment, bullying, discriminatory behavior; VMI civility training may be required
- Drug-free workplace certification (contracts >$10K)
- Immigration Reform and Control Act compliance (contracts >$10K)
- E-Verify program (contracts >$50K with 50+ employees)

## Data & Confidentiality
- PII of students/staff must be held confidential per federal law and Code of Virginia
- Breach notification required immediately to VMI
- VMI controls external reporting decisions
- Confidentiality statement may be required
- Data Protection Addendum (Attachment F)

## Internal Controls
- Contractor must demonstrate compliant internal controls for sensitive data
- SOC Reports or equivalent methodology required
- Deficiency reporting is mandatory

## Insurance Minimums
- Workers' Comp: statutory
- Employer's Liability: $100K
- Commercial General Liability: $1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate
- Auto Liability: $1M combined (if motor vehicle used)
- Commonwealth of Virginia added as additional insured

## Subcontracting
- No subcontracting without prior written VMI consent
- Subcontractor names, qualifications, experience must be furnished
- Prime remains fully liable

## Audit
- Records retained 5 years after final payment
- VMI/state auditors have full access

## IP & Ownership
- All data, materials, documentation belong exclusively to VMI
- Subject to Virginia FOIA (trade secrets can be protected per §2.2-4342F)

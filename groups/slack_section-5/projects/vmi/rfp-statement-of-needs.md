# Statement of Needs (Section V)

VMI seeks services to:
- Evaluate organizational systems
- Strengthen leadership development
- Strengthen group cohesiveness
- Support institutional effectiveness

## Minimum Requirements (contractor shall)

### Assessment & Methodology
1. Demonstrate experience conducting organizational assessments within higher education or similarly complex organizations
2. Administer a structured organizational assessment to evaluate:
   - Leadership practices
   - Communication systems
   - Organizational processes
   - External relationships
3. Utilize a survey-based assessment instrument capable of:
   - Collecting quantitative data
   - Benchmarking results against external datasets or comparable organizations

### Analysis & Reporting
4. Analyze assessment results and provide a written report:
   - Key findings
   - Strengths
   - Barriers to progress
   - Opportunities for improvement
5. Conduct structured engagement sessions or focus groups with selected cadets, staff, and faculty to gather qualitative feedback
6. Incorporate engagement session findings into overall analysis and recommendations
7. Develop a written report with:
   - Executive summary of findings
   - Strategic recommendations for leadership development, group cohesion, and organizational effectiveness

### Framework & Implementation
8. Provide a multi-year framework for implementation including:
   - Recommended initiatives
   - Performance indicators to measure progress over time
9. Provide leadership development education sessions for selected cadets, staff, and faculty to complement existing programming

### Advisory & Governance
10. Demonstrate experience establishing an internal advisory or leadership action group to support communication and engagement across the organization

### Ongoing Support
11. Provide guidance for ongoing monitoring and evaluation of the leadership development framework

## Response Requirements (Section VI.B)
1. RFP cover sheet + addenda acknowledgements
2. Plan and methodology for services (per Statement of Needs)
3. Written narrative: expertise, qualifications, experience of firm + resumes of key personnel
4. Offeror Data Sheet (Attachment C)
5. Small Business Subcontracting Plan (Attachment E) — required for contracts >$100K
6. VASCUPP member sales history (last 12 months)
7. Proposed Cost (Price Schedule, Section X)

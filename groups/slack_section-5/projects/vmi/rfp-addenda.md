## Key Strategic Takeaways

1. *Own methodology welcome* — OODA + BANI+ approach is explicitly permitted (Q1)
2. *No incumbent* — level playing field (Q8)
3. *4-year framework* — not 5 as initially assumed; proposal should cover 4 years (Q33)
4. *Consultant stays engaged* — multi-year relationship confirmed, not a hand-off (Q9)
5. *Assessment is not the end goal* — VMI wants observable change, not just reports (Q11)
6. *Survey once in Y1, then incoming class only* — reduces ongoing assessment burden (Q40)
7. *Travel reimbursed separately at gov rates* — keep out of fixed price (Q7, Q22)
8. *Phase-based Y1 pricing + annual fee for subsequent years* — pricing structure defined (Q6)
9. *AI may support but human-driven* — SurvAI delivery OK but frame as human-led (Q19)
10. *Existing committees to build on* — don't propose creating from scratch (Q34)
11. *Pre-existing IP protected* — BANI+, OODA, SurvAI, Leadership Circle all safe (Q24)
12. *SWaM not required but scored* — our team composition is a competitive advantage (Q44)
13. *Start date: late summer / early fall 2026* — timeline for mobilization planning (Q14)
14. *Security audits apply if storing VMI data* — factor into compliance plan (Q25)
15. *~1,750 total survey participants* — 1,500 cadets + 250 staff, confirmed twice (Q2, Q32)
